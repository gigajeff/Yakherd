# Independent Reviews

Store dated Red Team reports here. Reviews lead with findings and identify the
exact evidence/diff reviewed. Reviewers do not repair their targets. A review
may authorize a bounded promotion but does not become the product owner.

Use one active review path per strict-mode work ID. The initial review and one
recheck are the entire autonomous budget. Revise the record in place; do not
create version-suffixed candidates to evade the circuit breaker.
